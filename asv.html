ASV, or Anti-Snake Venom, is a lifesaving medicine that counteracts the venom injected during a snakebite. It works by targeting specific components of the snake venom, neutralizing their harmful effects.
What is Anti-Snake Venom (ASV)?
Unfortunately, access to ASV can be a challenge in some regions, particularly in developing countries. WFN's Life After Bite Mission advocates for increased access to ASV and works to raise awareness about its importance in saving lives.
Is ASV readily available?
Snake venom can cause serious health problems, including tissue damage, bleeding, and even death. By administering ASV promptly, doctors can significantly improve the chances of a full recovery after a snakebite.
